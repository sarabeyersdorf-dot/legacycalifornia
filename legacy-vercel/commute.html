§ IV · The other math

What you
get back.

The commute calculation is one side of the ledger. The other is everything you're driving toward. Numbers we don't usually quantify, but we should.

3,200
Avg. sqft of home · for the same money you spend on a Bay Area 1,200 sqft condo

The price-per-square-foot delta is the single biggest dollar conversion in foothills relocation. Most buyers more-than-double their footprint.

488
Hours of commute / yr · vs. 1,920 hrs at full RTO from Walnut Creek

Hybrid 2-day from Murphys is net less driving than full-RTO Walnut Creek. The geography is paradoxically efficient.

82°
Avg. summer high · Arnold & the higher Murphys parcels

The Bay's mid-August fog isn't a luxury you give up. The Sacramento Valley's 108° heat isn't one you take on. Foothills land between them.

14×
Per-acre land cost · Bay (median) vs. foothills (median)

If "more land" is on the wishlist at all, the change is closer to step-function than incremental. The math is unusual.
