  <div class="section-inner">

      <div class="story-side">
        <span class="hairline-brass"></span>
        <span class="eyebrow">Field notes · § I</span>
        <h2 class="h-section" style="margin-top:14px;">A four-block town that<br><em>punches well above</em> its weight.</h2>
      </div>
      <div class="story-body">
        <p class="lede">Founded by two Irish brothers in the spring of 1848, Murphys grew up around a creek, a wagon road, and the promise of gold. The gold thinned. The town didn't.</p>
        <p>Today, four blocks of Main Street hold more wine tasting rooms than most California towns thirty times its size — paired with a working hotel that's been pouring drinks since 1856, a stone-walled creekside park, and a quiet, fierce sense that the rest of the world can pass on through.</p>
        <p>Most of Legacy's clients come for the wine. They stay for the way Main Street still empties out by ten — and the way the sky over Murphys Park still holds every star you forgot you could see.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <!-- Personality strip: who fits here -->
