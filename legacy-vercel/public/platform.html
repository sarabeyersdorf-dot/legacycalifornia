The AI principle

Invisible, not absent.
Sara's voice. Always.

The platform's intelligence is in the background. Every message a buyer or seller receives is from Sara — never a generic drip, never a chat-bot. The AI writes drafts; Sara approves; clients feel the relationship.

I

Drafts are queued, not sent.

Every reply, follow-up, or sequence step is generated as a draft and held for Sara's review. She can edit, regenerate, or send as-is. Nothing leaves without her.

II

Sources are shown.

Every draft cites the signals it used — "pulled from your calendar" / "based on a call note from Mon." Highlighted phrases are hover-explainable. Trust is engineered in.

III

Triggers are quiet.

No drip blasts. Outreach fires only when a specific, observed behavior meets a rule (price-drop on a saved home, 3+ saves in a category, 14d silence on an active lead).

What it replaces

Two tools, one bill.
Goodbye to both.

Follow Up Boss handles your inbox. Ylopo runs the ads and feed. Neither knows about the other. Legacy puts them in one room, and adds the parts they don't do well — the seller portal, the editorial voice, the personal pace.
