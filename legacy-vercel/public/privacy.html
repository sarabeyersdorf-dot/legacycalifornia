Privacy policy.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Legacy Properties ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect through our website and property-search tools, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide on our forms: name, email address, phone number, and property preferences or questions you share with us.
  • Property activity such as listings you save, collections you view, tour requests, and feedback you leave, so we can serve your search.
  • Transaction information needed to manage your purchase or sale when you become a client.

How we use your information

We use your information only to respond to your inquiry, provide real estate services, share listings and market information you request, coordinate showings and appointments, and manage your transaction. We do not sell your personal information.

Text messaging (SMS)

  • No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties, excluding the aggregators and providers of the text-message services that deliver our messages.
  • We text only people who have given express consent. Message frequency varies with your activity — typically a few messages per week or fewer.
  • Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile plan.
  • Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time; reply HELP for help. Full program details are in our SMS messaging policy and SMS terms & conditions.

Sharing

We share information only as needed to provide our services — for example with escrow, title, lenders, and inspectors involved in your transaction, or with service providers that operate our website, email, and messaging on our behalf — and as required by law. We never sell or rent your personal information, and we never share your mobile number or SMS consent for marketing by others.

Retention and security

We keep client and transaction records for as long as California real estate law requires, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete your contact information, or opt out of texts (reply STOP) or emails (use the unsubscribe link) at any time. California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA; contact us to exercise them.
