§ I · The story

We moved here
before we worked here.

Sara and James moved to Angels Camp in 2014 from Walnut Creek, where Sara had spent a decade in commercial leasing and James had been a builder. The plan was three years and a remodel. Eight years later they're still here, in a different house, with a different kind of life — and a brokerage that grew out of how many of their friends started asking the same question: could you help us find one too?

In 2018 Sara got her broker's license and put up a single hand-set sign on a Murphys listing they'd quietly been helping a neighbor with. The phone rang. The phone kept ringing. Six months later they had a name on a door, a logo Sara had drawn in pencil on a coffee-shop napkin, and a promise to themselves: we will never grow this past the number we can do well.

That number turned out to be the two of them. It's still the two of them. Every showing, every contract, every closing letter — Sara or James, never a junior assistant, never an outsourced coordinator. When you work with Legacy, you work with the people whose names are on the wall.

§ II · What we believe

Four convictions
we don't break.

Stated out loud so you can hold us to them. If we drift, we drift toward these.

I

Volume is the enemy
of care.

Forty closings a year, between us, on purpose. Any more and we'd be doing the math instead of doing the work. Any less and we couldn't pay the bills.

II

No team, no juniors,
no handoffs.

Every text, email, walkthrough, and closing letter comes from Sara or James. The number on the website is the number that rings on our actual phone.

III

We tell you the
thing you didn't ask.

Septic, well, snow access, insurance, the neighbor's barking dog. The full picture, in the first conversation, before you're emotionally committed.

IV

The fee is the fee.
Once.

We're paid at closing, by one side, at a standard rate. No tiered services, no premium listings, no "we'll waive that for you" theater. Same deal for everyone.
